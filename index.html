Instructions

Concentration, also known as Match Match, Memory, Pelmanism, Shinkei-suijaku, Pexeso or simply Pais, is a card game in which all of the cards are laid face down on the surface and two cards are flipped face up over each turn. The object of the game is to turn over pairs of matching cards.

This is a Fun Game

You should play this game if you want to improve your memory skills. You know the old saying "use it or lose it!"
